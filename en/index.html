What SoulQ does

A small ritual, a deeper understanding

No need to write long journal entries. A few seconds a day is enough — and SoulQ starts revealing connections that would otherwise stay hidden.

Check in within seconds

Pick a mood, add a reason or a note, and you're done. Every entry is another step towards understanding yourself better.

Discover your patterns

SoulQ shows how things evolve over time and helps uncover the situations, relationships and habits that shape your wellbeing the most.

Reminders on your terms

Gentle reminders at a moment you choose. No spam. Just a little space to pause and check in with yourself.

Always with you

Your entries are safely stored and available on all your devices. They work online and offline.

We feel something every day. We rarely know why.

Work. School. Family. Relationships. Wins and worries. SoulQ helps you connect what you experience with how you feel — so you can better understand your inner world and make more conscious decisions.

Your data belongs to you

Records of how you feel are among the most personal information there is. That's why SoulQ has no ads, never sells your data, and gives you full control over what you share.
